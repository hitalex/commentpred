/////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////SPARK http://www.sparkawards.com//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////关于星火国际设计奖什么是星火国际设计奖星火国际设计奖是一个设计师和创意人的圈子，他们因有着共同的理念而走到一起——设计能够使世界产生积极而且重大的变化。而这种积极的理念的推广，则主要通过年度的国际设计比赛、展览、博客和专题讨论会等方式。星火国际设计奖覆盖多个学科，反应了当前多数设计者致力于团队合作这一现实。星火国家设计主张“我为人人”，努力从社会的各个阶层寻求最好的设计，无论是业余爱好者还是专业人士。星火国际设计奖把科技视为重要工具，因为它能构建一个更加富饶、创新型的社会。我们为何需要星火国际设计奖没有任何的团体或比赛能够规划如此广泛和宏大的日程安排。现在世界上有许多大规模的比赛，但是他们仅仅致力于商业推广、局域地区或是特定学科。星火国际设计奖支持多学科、全球性和积极进取的设计。星火团队的大多数人认为这是一种最现代的方式，并与他们本身关注社会的意识相符。谁支持星火国际设计奖星火国际设计奖热衷于与具有前瞻性思维的个体、学校和公司合作。星火国际设计奖咨询委员会、社区委员会和评判委员会包括了当今一批最顶级的设计师和最热手的年轻才俊。星火国际设计奖赞助商包括像惠普、欧特克这些为各个层次的设计师创造并推广了一系列的创新工具的公司。我们新的合伙伙伴-星火国际设计奖中国区，将会把星火国际设计奖团队扩展到这个设计迅速发展的国度，已经成为南美洲和英国合作关系的范例。星火国际设计奖与重点高校合作，协助发展联合促销、交流项目和前端研究。2009年包括加州艺术学院、广州美术学院、帕森设计学院、旧金山艺术学院、加州大学伯克利分校、视觉艺术学院和普拉特学院已经与我们建立了合作关系。星火国际设计奖与诸如澳大利亚Curve杂志、Core 77, Taxi, ArchNewsNow, Archinect 和 Yanko Design这样的权威媒体建立了紧密的联系。星火国际设计奖也以英国设计创新组织、全球设计网和香港设计师协会这些新的合作伙伴为荣。这是一个加强彼此合作和共享设计灵感的良好机遇。企业为何参与星火国际设计奖“设计铸就非凡“，开拓这一全新领域有助于公司扩大市场效应，使得公司内部的设计师紧跟设计理论与应用的最新潮流。星火国际设计奖帮助那些致力于环保设计的公司，并将这些设计推向全世界。专业设计师为何参与星火国际设计奖类似的方式，专业人士能够对世界范围内设计的进步产生更深的认识。他们能够目睹最前沿的设计，并在此影响下设计出自己的作品。只有少数的参赛者能够赢得星火国际设计奖大奖，但所有的参与者将会对这个互联的世界有更深刻的理解。大多数设计师认为最关键的“将作品传播出去”，星火国际设计奖也将为此创造最现代的环境。学生为何参与星火国际设计奖青年学生是充满活力的栋梁之材，加以时日，必将成为参天大树。星火国际设计奖比赛为青年设计师提供宝贵的现代设计环境，能使他们的努力得到肯定。学生已经成为星火国际设计奖获得者的重要组成部分-其中不乏经典之作。星火国际设计奖和中国星火国际设计奖为什么会在中国出现当星火的创建者第一次见到中国的伙伴时，它在中国的光明前景就已经显而易见。作为一个名副其实的国际奖项，星火必须深深扎根于中国。对我们这个团体来说，这是一个互相联系、共同学习的机遇，造福于我们亲爱的地球母亲。我们知道，这是一个长期发展的过程，所以我们建立了良好的远程运行体制。我们的力量会接受考验，但这是值得的，将会有助于增进理解和促进更好设计的诞生。中国设计师能从星火国际设计奖获得什么这是一个体验设计新潮流的机会，有助于形成一个支持和促进最新设计观念的团体。它不仅仅是中国的、美国的、欧洲的，而是世界性的。ABOUT SPARKWhat is SPARK?Spark is first and foremost a community of designers and creative people, bound together by the idea that Design can make significant, positive changes in the world and help make it better. The way we promote this good design is through the annual organization of international design competitions, exhibitions, blogs and workshops. Spark is multi-disciplinary, reflecting the modern fact that most designers work on teams in this fashion. Spark is also “One For All,” in that we seek out the best designs from all levels of society, from amateurs to the most experienced. Spark sees technology as a great enabling tool, that is helping all of society to be more creative, productive and innovative.Why we need SPARKNo other group or competition pursues this broad and ambitious agenda. There are many fine, major competitions, but they tend to be narrowly focused on commercial promotion, regional representation or specific disciplines. Spark is aspirational, international and trans-discipline. Many in the Spark community consider this the most contemporary approach, one that agrees with their own socially-aware sensibility.Who supports SPARK? Spark has always enjoyed the friendship and partnership of forward-thinking individuals, schools and companies. The Spark Advisory Council, community boards and Juries include some of the greatest of designers and some of the hottest young talent. Spark corporate support includes companies like Hewlett-Packard and Autodesk, who create and promote a vast range of creative tools for every level of designer. Our new partnership – Spark China – will extend the Spark community to this fast-growing design scene, and is already the model for similar partnerships in Latin America and the UK.Spark works with major schools and universities, assisting in co-promotion of events, interchange programs and advanced studies. In 2009 these schools included California College of Art, Guangzhou Academy of Fine Art, Parsons New School of Design, Academy of Art San Francisco, UC Berkeley, School of Visual Arts and Pratt Institute.Spark has strong relations with important design media like Curve Magazine, Core 77, Taxi, ArchNewsNow, Archinect and Yanko Design. Spark is also very proud of it's new association partners, the British Design Innovation Organization, Global Design Network and the Hong Kong Designers Association. This is an opportunity for increased collaboration and sharing of design DNA.Why companies join SPARKTo explore the new space that “Design that Makes a Difference” can offer their marketing efforts. This can help their in-house designers keep in touch with the latest trends in design theory and application. Spark also helps companies that are making a serious effort in eco-conscious design to communicate that to the world.Why professionals join SparkIn similar fashion, professionals gain greater understanding of progress being achieved in the world of Better Design. They can see the most modern work, and have their own work judged in context to it. Only a very few entrants will win a top Spark! Award, but all who take part gain a deeper understanding of inter-connected world. The key thing most designers agree on—that it’s very important to “get the work out there.” Spark provides the most modern context for that work.Why students should join SPARKStudents are the young green trees that will grow to mighty size, with time and the right experience, Exposure to the Spark competitions and community offers a valuable modern context for young designers, and helps them get recognition for their efforts. Already, student entries make up a significant portion of Spark winners—and some of its best work.SPARK AND CHINAWhy SPARK began in China When the founders of Spark first met it’s future partners and new friends in China, it immediately became obvious. To be a truly international Award, Spark must develop strong roots in China. It is an opportunity for our communities to reach out to each other, learn about working together and do good things for our dear Earth. We understand that this will be a process that develops over time, as we create systems that function well over great distances. We know that our strength will be tested, but we also know that this is worthwhile and will contribute to understanding and better design solutions.What could Chinese designers gain from SPARKAn opportunity to take part in a great new wave of design, and to help form a community that supports and promotes a strong and modern design consciousness. Not Chinese, not American, not European—but worldwide. http://sparkawards.citiexpo.com/file/news07.html
